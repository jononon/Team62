About Team 62

Team 62 is an independant VRC team that competes on the regional, national, and international levels. We have been very successful over the past two years. We have two teams, The first being 62, which is based out of Glendale, CA. And The Second, 62 B, which is based out of Rancho Palos Verdes, CA.
